# Release Notes: Staff-Entry Indicator Improvements

- Staff-entered percentage warning now triggers at 5% instead of 6%
- Percentage display uses proper rounding (4.9% no longer shows as 4%)

- Hovering the pill shows the precise percentage (one decimal place) in the tooltip card

## For Users 👤
- The staff-entered pill in the header will now catch your attention sooner (at 5%) with a warm amber glow
